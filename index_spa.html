ABOUT US

We are a family owned and operated company servicing the Inland NW and Seattle areas.

Caribou Rentals and Leasing was formed out of the desire to bring a better trailer rental experience to the Spokane and Inland NW area. With large chains dominating the market we felt it was time to offer that personal touch where customers aren’t just a number that can be moved around.

We set out with one key goal: when we say we’ll rent you something on a certain day at a certain time, you will be able to pickup as planned. We won’t send you to another location 2 hours away. We won’t tell you to wait for it to be returned by another customer. It will be ready for you. You made a commitment to us, and we will hold up our end of the bargain.

We decided we can do better than that though. We can bring the same family oriented and customer focused philosophy to longer term leases. And not just with trailers, but with various equipment you may need to rent or lease as well. We do not yet have a large variety of equipment, but we look forward to meeting our customers, hearing about what they need, and helping to make that equipment available.

Your small business, your projects, your to-do lists, and your time matter to us and we genuinely want to help you accomplish what you need.

Ultimately, we focus on being considerate of our customers while providing personable, friendly service, striving to keep everyone coming back to Caribou because we make renting and leasing trailers and equipment a more dependable and enjoyable experience.
